Understanding Risk Levels

Our AI model classifies customers into risk categories to help you prioritize retention efforts

HIGH RISK

Churn Likely

Customers classified as high risk show strong indicators of potential churn and require immediate attention.

  • Low engagement and satisfaction scores
  • Decreased order frequency
  • Multiple complaints registered
  • Long gaps since last purchase
  • Minimal app usage time
LOW RISK

Retain Customer

Customers in this category demonstrate healthy engagement patterns and are likely to continue their relationship.

  • High satisfaction and engagement
  • Regular purchase patterns
  • Active app usage
  • Positive interaction history
  • Strong loyalty indicators
